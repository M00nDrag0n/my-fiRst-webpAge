Did You Know?

  • Flying foxes are the largest bat species.
  • Most bats have a diet of fruit, flying insects, and small animals like mice and rats.
  • Only vampire bats feed on blood!
  • Watch out for rabies!
  • As far as we know, Flying Foxes do not like Twizzlers.
